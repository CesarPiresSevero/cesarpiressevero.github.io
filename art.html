Literature

O menino do parquinho

Josiah, 11 years old, average kid is joining his parents on a Sunday afternoon groceries shopping. Little did he know, he would finally find what he was looking for. O menino do parquinho (The boy from the playground) is a partial self bibliography, detailing the childhood of the author. The book carries a lot of symbolisms, which the reader must be quite attentive to grasp. This book is only available in Portuguese.
