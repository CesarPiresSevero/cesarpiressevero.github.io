Hey, I'm Cesar!

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a project manager with a love for philosophy, music, nature and learning. I thrive on challenges, constantly looking for the next one. I'm always eager to learn, create, and share my experiences.

My journey

From a small town in the south of Brazil, I was able to visit many places in the world. Living in Brazil, South Korea and Sweden, provided me with the experience to understand different cultures. This understanding became part of me. I carried a little bit of each place I've been. This way, I can't really say where I'm from besides planet Earth!

Career & Expertise

I started my career as a electric engineer. I had the opportunity to work in many areas, such as automation, sales, software development and then finally project management. I'm blessed to have the chance to work with people all over the world. In solo projects, start-ups and worldwide companies. Currently, I live in Sweden, working for one of the biggest companies in the world.

Passions

Most of my free time is spent reading, coding, playing the piano and writing books. Occasionally, I do enjoy playing some Nintendo games. I've read many books on philosophy, politics and Victorian era in general. I like to create new songs, having played the guitar for many years. Lately, I've been getting deeper into piano and voice. When the mood is right, I enjoy writing books, thus externalizing emotions and thoughts into characters that can embody my eccentricity.
